# 📖 Signature Platform: The Complete User Guide

Welcome to **Signature**! This guide will walk you through every feature of the platform, from your first login to sharing your professional URL with the world.

---

## 🏗️ 1. Setting Up Your Account

To begin, you need a secure workspace.

* **Sign-In Options:** We support **Google**, **GitHub**, and traditional **Email/Password** authentication.
* **The Dashboard:** Once logged in, your dashboard gives you a bird's-eye view of your portfolio status (Published vs. Draft) and quick links to the Editor.

---

## ✍️ 2. Using the Portfolio Editor

The Editor is split into logical sections. You can access these via the sidebar.

### 👤 About Me

This is your "Hero" section.

* **Profile Image:** Upload a high-quality headshot (square aspect ratio works best).
* **Bio:** Keep it professional yet personal. Mention what you do and what drives you.
* **Resume:** Upload your CV in PDF format. A "Download Resume" button will automatically appear in your portfolio header.

### 💼 Experience & Education

* **Chronology:** Add your work history and academic background.
* **Reordering:** Use the **Drag-and-Drop** handles (⋮) to move items up or down. Usually, the most recent experience should be at the top.

### 🚀 Projects & GitHub Sync

You can showcase your work in two ways:

1. **Manual Entry:** Upload a thumbnail, add a title, description, and links to the live demo.
2. **GitHub Import:** Enter your GitHub username to fetch your repositories. Signature will pull in your project description, primary language, and star count automatically.

### 🛠️ Skills

* **Categories:** Group your skills (e.g., "Frontend," "DevOps," "Soft Skills").
* **Proficiency:** You can set levels for each skill to show recruiters your strengths.

---

## 🎨 3. Customizing Your "Personality"

This is what makes Signature unique. Your "Personality" defines the entire visual language of your site.

### Selecting a Theme

Navigate to **Theme Settings** to choose one of our five core identities:

* **Minimal:** Best for corporate or high-end professional roles.
* **Developer:** Features a code-editor aesthetic, monospace fonts, and a "Terminal" feel.
* **Creative:** Bold colors and artistic layouts for designers and creators.
* **Elegant:** Focused on clean white space and sophisticated serif typography.
* **Futuristic:** High-tech 3D backgrounds and neon accents (highly interactive).

### Visual Fine-Tuning

* **Accent Color:** Choose a primary color that reflects your brand.
* **Layout Order:** Use the **Layout Editor** to rearrange entire sections. For example, if you are a fresh graduate, you might want "Education" to appear before "Experience."
* **Spacing (Density):** Choose *Compact* for a data-rich view or *Spacious* for a modern, airy feel.

---

## 👁️ 4. Preview & Responsive Testing

Before going live, use the **Preview Panel**:

* **Device Toggle:** Switch between **Desktop**, **Tablet**, and **Mobile** icons to ensure your content looks perfect on every screen.
* **Real-time Updates:** Any text change you make in the editor updates the preview instantly—no refreshing required.

---

## 🌐 5. Publishing & Domain

* **Claiming your Username:** Navigate to **Settings** and choose a unique username (e.g., `ayman-dev`).
* **Public Link:** Your portfolio will be accessible at `yoursite.com/username`.
* **Visibility Toggle:** You can set your portfolio to **Private** while you are working on it. Once you flip the switch to **Published**, your link becomes active for the world to see.

---

## ❓ Troubleshooting & Tips

* **Images not appearing?** Ensure your image files are under 5MB for optimal loading speeds.
* **GitHub Sync failing?** Make sure your GitHub profile and the specific repositories you want to show are set to **Public**.
* **SEO:** Signature automatically generates meta tags for your portfolio so you appear in Google search results.
